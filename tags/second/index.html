Bengal

The Royal Farman of 1717 allowed the company Duty free trade in bengal and permits for such goods, company servants could also trade, but were not extended the farman. The government under [[Murshid Quli Khan]] saw loss of revenue because of the farman hence the company was required to deposit a lumpsum to the treasury. The company also misused dastak(exempt from paying customs duty) [[Alivardi Khan]] also paid close attention to the actions of British and their rivalry with French, he did not let the British fortify their settlements....
